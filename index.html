What We Do?

The full service we provide is
designed to meet your startup needs.

Pitch Deck Forming

If you can't find time to prepare a pitch deck due to the workload of your startup or you want to prepare it with a professional, you are at the right address. Our SLA is fantastic; your pitch deck will be ready within two days workshop. You don't have to waste your time, just focus on your startup and business targets.

Annual Support

Well, your pitch deck is ready, but you have made some critical changes on your products or services during the year, or the investors have just asked you for some changes. Don't worry, we are still here! We don't give our support just once; we carry out the changes in your startup on the pitch deck together each quarter throughout the whole year.

Negotiation Mentoring

What if one day an investor wants to invest in your startup and calls you, how would you start? How much investment can you get and how many shares do you plan to give in return? We also provide negotiation consultancy in meetings for your investment interviews. We are with you throughout the year, with 1-hour mentoring meetings in each quarter.

Corporate Solutions

We support not only startups but also corporate companies. You can get Boot Camp Workshop Packages for all your existing and new startups in your Incubation/Acceleration center. If you do not have an Incubation/Acceleration center, we can just create it on behalf of your company or carry out your work in your existing center to create added value for your startups.
